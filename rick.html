Rick A. Kosinski, CP

Send an Email to Mr. Kosinski

President

Mr. Kosinski is the owner of Williams Aerial & Mapping, Inc. and its subsidiaries. He is an ASPRS Certified Photogrammetrist and is a Commercial/ Instrument/ Multi-Engine rated pilot with over 3000 hours logged as a photo pilot. Mr. Kosinski has over 20 years experience in all facets of photogrammetry, photo lab, surveying, and GIS. Including: Aerial survey pilot for 10 years, aerial camera system operator for 10 years, stereo plotter operator 4 years, photographic lab work for 8 years, and 8 years experience in GPS control, and geographic information systems. Mr. Kosinski is responsible for our customer service system, which ensures consistent communication and satisfaction with our clients.

Mr. Kosinski's professional experience includes:

  • Flight Deptartment Manager & Company pilot - Williams Aerial & Mapping, Inc.
  • General Manager - Williams Aerial & Mapping, Inc.
  • Flight Department Manager & Company pilot Air-Land Surveys, Inc.
  • Aerial Camera System Operator (Wild RC8, RC20, RC30 & Zeiss Jena)
  • Stereo Plotter Operator (5000 hours in 4 years)
  • AutoCad System Operator - 3 years

    • Education:

  • ASPRS Measuring the Earth II - Fall 2006
  • KORK Digital Mapping System Training - 1992
  • Coyote Worksop (LMK2000) - 1991 & 1992
  • Leica & Zeiss Workshops - 1993 & 1996

    • Certifications:

  • Certified Photogrammetrist - American Society for Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing (ASPRS), Certification Number 1339
  • Certified Commercial Pilot - 1988
