Zeiss /Jena LMK2000


The LMK is a large format mapping camera manufactured by Zeiss Jena in the former East Germany. After the reunification of Germany, the company was taken over by Carl Zeiss, Inc. and production of aerial cameras in Jena was discontinued.

Williams Aerial & Mapping, Inc. was the first private company to purchase the Zeiss /Jena LMK2000 in 1991. Williams uses a gyroscopically stabilized mount for our LMK2000. Combined with the camera's forward motion compensation capabilities, we are able to ensure high quality, sharp and perfectly vertical imagery at many shutter speeds. We continually maintain our sensors and have them USGS-Certified. Click the link to view our LMK2000 camera report.

Our Background with the Zeiss /Jena LMK2000


The LMK2000 has been Williams go to camera for years. Our processing team is equally experienced as well. We pride ourselves in processing the data for your job in house with a highly trained and experienced team. With a total of over 60 years of photogrammetry background, our team will develop a solution to fit any requirements for your project.

Williams Aerial & Mapping, Inc. has a rich history in the creation of planimetric and topographic maps. Beginning as the photogrammetry department with Clyde E. Williams and Associates, the company had it's origins in 1941. Today Williams is a thriving firm of Professional Photogrammetrists. We look forward to hearing from you as you search out the right fit for your project.
