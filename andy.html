Vice President

Mr. Williams is the Vice President of the company, his areas of expertise are in the fields of digital imaging and flight direction. He overseas the production of digital imagery from scanning to orthorectification and delivery. He has led the development of our proprietary system of image mosaicking and quality control and assurance to insure the highest standard of quality for all of the orthophotography production. Mr. Williams has been involved with photogrammetry and surveying for over 20 years.

His experience includes; senior topographic mapping editor, stereo compilation, civil engineering design CAD, field survey technician, camera systems operator, and data conversion manager.

Mr. Williams professional experience includes:

  • Camera System Operator - Williams Aerial & Mapping, Inc. - July, 2004
  • Digital Image Manager - Williams Aerial & Mapping, Inc. - 8 years
  • Topographic Mapping Editor - Clyde E. Williams & Associates, Inc. - 9 years
  • Camera Systems Operator - Clyde E. Williams & Associates, Inc. - 3 years
  • Stereo Plotter Operator - Clyde E. Williams & Associates - 3 years
  • Field Survey Technician - Clyde E. Williams & Associates, Inc. - 2 years
  • Engineering Desaign CAD - Clyde E. Williams & Associates, Inc. - 5 years
